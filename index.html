Pokemon Bar Chart

The bar graph featured in this data visualization provides a comprehensive view of the distribution of attack, defense, and stamina stats among the Pokémon from the Kanto region. The graph allows you to effortlessly explore the variations in these crucial attributes across the 151 Pokémon. With the added functionality of a dynamic button, you have the power to switch between the distributions of attack, defense, and stamina with a simple click. Moreover, as you navigate through the graph, a delightful interactive feature allows you to hover over individual bars, highlighting them to gain deeper insights into specific Pokémon and their corresponding stat values.

Choose the stat to compare!

Network of Pokemon Types

In this captivating network graph, exploring the type weaknesses of Pokémon from the Kanto region, hovering over a node reveals the weaknesses of that specific Pokémon. The nodes connect if that pokemon's type is in the weakness of the other connected pokemon. As you hover, the graph dynamically displays the vulnerabilities, providing instant insights into the strategic aspects of battles. Discover the hidden weaknesses of each Pokémon with a simple hover, enhancing your understanding of the interplay between types.

Map of Pokemon Locations

Welcome to the captivating map graph, where the world map comes alive with points representing the locations where Pokémon were found in the Kanto region. This visually engaging graph provides a bird's-eye view of Pokémon distributions across the globe, letting you zoom into the action. Each point on the map corresponds to a specific Pokémon, with different colors indicating the various types of Pokémon. Uncover the geographical nuances and hotspots that define the Kanto region's Pokémon as you embark on an exciting journey through this visually immersive map graph.
